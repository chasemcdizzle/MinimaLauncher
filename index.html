what
MinimaLauncher is a mini Wii/vWii homebrew application that launches a game that is inserted into the disc slot. Of course, the game has to be a Wii or Gamecube game, and GC will not work on vWii. It takes the beautiful nature of being minimal and the outcome is you playing your game from whatever region, with whatever cheats, at utmost ease.

Initially, this was made to launch Super Smash Bros. Brawl with the SDHC extension code, but I figured I should let it be a new way of loading any Wii/GC game with any code you may desire. It is open source and available at my github

How?
Place the files inside of your apps folder. Your folder layout should now appear as: SD:\apps\Minimalauncher\ Within that folder should be:
  • boot.dol
  • meta.xml
  • icon.png
Run the homebrew channel and click load.

It also works with those of you that use hackless loading. Put the boot.elf on the root of your sd card and boom! It's that easy, the game should now be up and running.
